SCENE 8: "Exposure"
Setting: A detention cell deep in Khalid's estate. Clinical but wrong — reinforced walls, restraint mounts, a drain in the floor. Medical-grade monitoring equipment beside devices that aren't medical. A sealed tank on a trolley near the door.
Characters: Zephyr, Khalid, Cassius, Erik Lindqvist
POV: Zephyr

CHARACTER REFERENCE:
  Cassius: Caught operating for the underground. Beaten. Chained to the wall. He does NOT know Erik is underground. He DOES know the resistance network, the code phrase, and Zephyr's real identity. All of this will be extracted.
  Erik: Forced to administer the interrogation. Underground member operating the mind-extraction on his own ally. Cannot react. Professional mask is the only thing keeping him alive.
  Zephyr: Watching his friend be destroyed. Cannot intervene. Must perform total submission to protect his cover.
  Khalid: Furious. A spy in his household is a personal betrayal. Wants to know everything.

NOTE: The squalid extracts memories and replays them. The subject experiences a state of pure bliss during extraction — like a sustained orgasm. Once the squalid is separated, the subject cannot function. This is the fast version of what pheromone addiction does slowly. The extraction takes approximately 32 hours. This creates a hard deadline for the climax — the underground must act before the memories are fully replayed.

---

NARRATION: A detention cell. Reinforced walls, strip lighting, a drain set into the metal floor. Cassius is chained to the far wall — arms above his head, wrists raw. His face is swollen. One eye half-shut. Blood crusted at the corner of his mouth. He's been here for hours.
NARRATION: The door opens. Khalid walks in first. Zephyr follows. Erik enters last, data pad in hand — summoned as the household's medical authority. The only one certified to operate bio-extraction.
NARRATION: Zephyr sees Cassius and stops. Just for a second. His breath catches. Then he keeps walking.
INTERNAL: No. No no no. Not him. Not Cassius.
NARRATION: Cassius hangs from the chains. He doesn't look up. He doesn't look at Zephyr. His one good eye stays fixed on the floor.
[Khalid] I've been told we have a spy in the household.
[Cassius] (hoarse) I'm not a spy. They took me. Your guards. I wasn't doing anything. They beat me.
NARRATION: Khalid walks over to him. Backhands him across the face. Cassius's head snaps sideways. Blood spatters the wall.
[Khalid] Admit it.
[Cassius] I'm not lying. I promise. Please. You have to believe me. I didn't do anything.
[Khalid] Shut him up. I don't want to hear this.
NARRATION: Erik steps forward. Pulls a gag from the restraint kit on the wall. Fastens it around Cassius's head. His hands are steady. His face gives nothing.
[Khalid] (turning to Zephyr) Zephyr — do you know this attendant?
[Zephyr] I don't—
[Khalid] (shouting) Do you know this attendant?
[Zephyr] He came to me. Last night. During the blackout. He said he recognised me. From Ionion. He wanted to see how I was doing. That's all.
[Khalid] That's all? He's a fucking SPY!
[Zephyr] I promise, Sir. That's all he said to me.
[Khalid] You'd better not be lying. Bring in the squalid.
NARRATION: Erik types something into his data pad. His thumb pauses for a fraction of a second over the confirmation key. Then presses it.
[Erik] (flat) They're on their way.
[Khalid] (to Zephyr) And you. You tell me whenever someone speaks to you. Anyone. You understand?
[Zephyr] I didn't know, Sir. I'm sorry.
[Khalid] Yes. You'd better be sorry. Get here.
NARRATION: Zephyr walks to Khalid. Khalid grabs the back of his neck.
[Khalid] On your knees.
NARRATION: Zephyr drops to his knees. Khalid unzips and pisses on him. It runs down Zephyr's face, through his hair, soaking his shirt. Zephyr opens his mouth. Swallows. Doesn't flinch.
NARRATION: Cassius hangs from the wall. His one good eye is open. He's not looking at Zephyr. He's staring at the ceiling. Refusing to watch.
[Zephyr] Thank you, Sir. May I please kiss your boots?
[Khalid] No.
NARRATION: Khalid pulls Zephyr to his feet by the collar. The door opens. An attendant rolls in a sealed tank on a trolley. The tank is filled with dark liquid. Something moves inside — slow, coiling shapes pressing against the glass.
[Khalid] (to Cassius) Now you see how your prince is doing. And what do you say?
NARRATION: Cassius says nothing behind the gag. His eye stays on the ceiling.
[Khalid] I want to know everything.
[Erik] Of course.
NARRATION: Khalid grabs Zephyr's head. Turns it toward Cassius. Forces him to face the wall.
[Khalid] Now. I want you to watch. See what happens to those from the world you once knew.
[Zephyr] He isn't anyone. I don't know him. He's just an attendant.
[Khalid] We'll soon find out. Last chance — tell me what he told you. And I'll stop it.
[Zephyr] Nothing. I told you everything. I swear.
[Khalid] Very well. Get on with it.
NARRATION: Erik approaches the tank. Unseals the lid. The smell hits first — salt and something organic, deep-sea rot. He takes an electric prod from the trolley and touches the surface of the liquid.
NARRATION: The creature rises. Pale, translucent, the size of a large dog. Tentacles — dozens of them — unfurl from a central mass, each one tipped with something that pulses faintly. It pulls itself over the rim of the tank, leaving a trail of thick slime on the metal floor.
NARRATION: It moves toward Cassius. Slow. Deliberate. Tentacles testing the air ahead of it. The slime hisses faintly where it touches the drain grate.
NARRATION: The creature reaches Cassius's feet. Begins to climb. Tentacles wrap around his legs, his torso, sliding under his clothes, finding skin. Cassius's body goes rigid against the chains.
NARRATION: It pulls the gag away with one tentacle. Cassius's mouth opens.
[Cassius] (screaming) Fucking imperial! We'll—
NARRATION: A tentacle slides into his mouth. Another into each ear. The central mass rises and envelops his head. Cassius's body convulses against the chains — violent, thrashing. Then stops. Goes completely limp.
NARRATION: His body changes. The tension drains out of him. His hands unclench. His breathing deepens. A sound comes from behind the mass covering his face — not pain. Something else. A low moan. His back arches. His fingers spread wide.
INTERNAL: He's not screaming anymore. He's — no. No, that's not — that sound. What is it doing to him?
NARRATION: Erik walks over. Scans the creature and Cassius with a handheld device. His face is stone.
[Khalid] How long?
[Erik] At least thirty-two hours for full extraction.
[Khalid] Good.
NARRATION: Khalid lets go of Zephyr's head. Zephyr doesn't look away. Cassius's body hangs slack in the chains, the creature pulsing slowly around his head. His chest rises and falls in deep, rhythmic waves. Another low sound — bliss where there should be agony.
INTERNAL: That's what they'll do to me. Not the pain. The pleasure. They don't need to hurt you. They just need to make you want it. And then you can never want anything else.
NARRATION: Khalid walks to the door. Doesn't look back.
[Khalid] (from the doorway) Come, Zephyr. Leave the overseer to his work.
NARRATION: Zephyr follows. In the doorway, he stops. Cassius's one visible hand — the left, hanging loose from the chain — twitches once. The fingers curl into a fist. Then relax. Zephyr doesn't know if it was deliberate.
NARRATION: Erik stands over Cassius with his scanner. He doesn't look up as they leave. The door seals behind them.
