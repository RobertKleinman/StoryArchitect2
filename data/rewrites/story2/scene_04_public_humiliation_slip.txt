NARRATION: Khalid lounges on the victor's dais, legs extended. His boots are off. The crowd's roar dips to a hungry murmur. {Khalid's soles loom inches from Zephyr's face, glistening with fresh sweat.}
[OBIORA ACHEBE] (commanding_amusement) (deep rumble) Kneel proper, boy. Show 'em how a fringe rat honors his champion. {He flexes his toes, a bead of sweat rolls down the arch.}
[RAFAEL ESPINOZA] (sarcastic_tense) (quick lilt) Yeah, yeah—proper as the senate's promises. Right here? {Zephyr drops to his knees in the hot sand, hands hovering near Khalid's ankle.}
INTERNAL (dizzy_conflict): That smell hits first—thick, like engine grease mixed with salt. Pulls at something low in my gut.
[OBIORA ACHEBE] (dry_wit) (rumbling command) Tongue out. Lick the grit off. Earn your keep. {His foot shifts forward, pressing the ball against Zephyr's lips.}
[RAFAEL ESPINOZA] (deflecting_humiliated) (breathless quip) Grit from the pit, huh? Bet it's got stories— {Zephyr's tongue darts out, tracing the sweat-slick curve. The musk floods his nose, heavy and unyielding.}
NARRATION: Crowd holo-grids magnify the act. Cheers swell. Zephyr's hands grip Khalid's calf, slick with arena heat. {Sweat drips from Khalid's sole onto Zephyr's chin.}
INTERNAL (hazy_resistance): Just a tool. Info from his whispers later. But the taste—salty, clinging. Why's my head swimming?
[OBIORA ACHEBE] (controlled_gentleness) (low growl) Deeper. Between the toes. Like you mean it. {He spreads his toes wide, trapping Zephyr's nose in the damp crevice.}
[RAFAEL ESPINOZA] (sarcastic_dazed) (speeding slur) Mean it? Like you'd mean a veto on Cassius's bill? {Zephyr presses in, tongue working the webbing. Musk coils thicker, eyes watering.}
NARRATION: Zephyr sways. His knee slips on a puddle of mingled sweat. He pitches forward, faceplanting into the sand beside Khalid's foot. {Sand sticks to his sweat-smeared cheek. Crowd erupts in laughter.}
[OBIORA ACHEBE] (stoic_irritation) (curt order) Up. Now. {His hand clamps Zephyr's shoulder, hauling him back to position. No smile.}
[RAFAEL ESPINOZA] (embarrassed_defiant) (sharp quip) Slippery down here. Your mess, champ. {Zephyr scrambles up, wiping sand from his mouth, but leans back in.}
INTERNAL (resilient_shame): Laughter everywhere. Cassius wouldn't slip like this. Cassius's plotting real pain, not... this haze.
NARRATION: Khalid's foot presses down again. Crowd jeers echo. Zephyr's gaze flicks to the senate hologrid—Cassius's shadowed face watches. {A loose comm bead rolls from Zephyr's collar into the sand.}
[OBIORA ACHEBE] (rising_volume) (booming) Finish it. Eyes on me.
[RAFAEL ESPINOZA] (dazed_resolute) (trailing off) Yeah... finishing. {He laps once more, then pulls back as Khalid withdraws.}
