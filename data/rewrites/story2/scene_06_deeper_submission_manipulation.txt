SCENE 6: "Deeper Submission"
Setting: Dining room aboard Khalid's personal cruiser — traveling FTL. A viewport shows the warp field. Intimate space, not grand. This is Khalid's private ship, not a military vessel.
Characters: Zephyr, Khalid (General's aide briefly on comms)
POV: Zephyr

CHARACTER REFERENCE:
  Zephyr: The chemical pull has been quieter since Erik's injection — though Zephyr doesn't know why. He can think more clearly. And he's still here. That's worse.
  Khalid: Away from the capital, on his own ship, traveling to his holdings. More relaxed than we've seen him. The public persona drops. What's underneath is more complicated than Zephyr expected.

NOTE: Zephyr does NOT know the injection was a counteragent. He just notices the craving feels different — quieter, less overwhelming. He doesn't understand why. And he's still choosing to be here.

---

NARRATION: A dining room aboard a cruiser. Small, private. A viewport fills one wall — the warp field streams past in ribbons of blue and white. Khalid sits at the table, one hand on a comm panel, mid-conversation. Zephyr enters. Khalid raises a finger without looking at him. Zephyr drops to his knees by the door. Waits.
[Khalid] (into comm) And you make sure Titus understands. I'm done talking to him.
[Aide] He's being stubborn, my Lord.
[Khalid] Fucking idiot.
[Aide] You know what he wants.
[Khalid] I have a guest. Make him change his mind.
NARRATION: Khalid kills the comm. Sits back. Looks at Zephyr kneeling by the door.
[Khalid] Come here. Sit.
NARRATION: He gestures to the chair across from him. Zephyr stands but walks behind Khalid first. Puts his hands on Khalid's shoulders. Starts working the muscles.
[Zephyr] Titus. Is everything alright?
[Khalid] (changing the subject) I'm told you wanted to see me.
[Zephyr] Yes, Sir.
[Khalid] You don't know how happy that makes me.
NARRATION: Khalid reaches up and takes hold of Zephyr's hands. Brings them to his lips. Kisses them. It's gentle. Not a performance.
[Zephyr] Me too, Sir.
[Khalid] Go sit.
NARRATION: Zephyr sits across from him. Khalid taps something on a keypad built into the table.
[Khalid] Didn't I tell you that you'd understand your place.
[Zephyr] Yes, Sir.
[Khalid] We all eventually understand our place.
[Zephyr] Yes, Sir.
[Khalid] Speak freely. I want to know what you really think.
NARRATION: The door opens. Two attendants enter with trays — grilled meat, something green and steaming, bread that smells like it was baked an hour ago. They set it on the table in silence and leave. The door seals behind them.
[Khalid] Eat. This is for you.
NARRATION: Zephyr picks up a fork. The first bite stops him. Rich, tender, nothing like the nutrient packs and slop from the barracks. He eats faster than he means to.
[Zephyr] It's so good. Thank you.
[Khalid] (smiling) See? I'm not that bad.
[Zephyr] I never thought that, Sir.
[Khalid] Ha! It's what you all think. Isn't it?
[Zephyr] I just want to serve, Sir.
[Khalid] Really?
NARRATION: Khalid leans back. Studies him.
[Khalid] What was it like? On Ionion.
[Zephyr] It was...
NARRATION: Zephyr's fork pauses. His jaw tightens.
[Khalid] Tell me. I promise. Don't be afraid.
[Zephyr] It was fine. I was sent to the Imperial Academy on Tarion when I was sixteen.
[Khalid] So you're smarter than you look?
[Zephyr] No. No, it was my family. They arranged it.
[Khalid] I told you to be honest with me, boy. What did you study?
[Zephyr] AI systems and mathematics.
[Khalid] And what did you really want?
[Zephyr] Not this.
[Khalid] Ha! There's some honesty. There's some spunk.
[Zephyr] Sorry, Sir.
[Khalid] No. No. I told you to be honest.
NARRATION: A silence. The warp field pulses outside the viewport. Zephyr sets his fork down.
[Zephyr] What about you, Sir?
[Khalid] Me?
[Zephyr] Yes, Sir. I want to know about you.
NARRATION: Khalid doesn't answer right away. He turns his chair toward the viewport. The blue light catches the scars on his neck. He looks tired in a way Zephyr hasn't seen before.
[Khalid] Isn't it beautiful?
[Zephyr] It is, Sir.
NARRATION: Zephyr gets up. Moves behind Khalid again. Hands on his shoulders, working the tension out of the muscle. Khalid doesn't stop him. The warp field streams past.
[Khalid] I was eleven when the Empire came to our world. There was no notice. No warning. Orbital bombardment first. Then the ground troops. My parents got me out — put me on a ship. Whoever would take children. Anyone.
NARRATION: He pauses. His hand comes up to rest on Zephyr's forearm. Not gripping. Just resting.
[Khalid] Turns out it was a slaver. My parents didn't know. But the twelve of us children figured it out quickly enough. He had us in a cargo hold with a locked hatch. Fed us twice a day. We were stock.
[Zephyr] (quiet) I'm sorry.
[Khalid] Don't be. One night he drank himself stupid. Passed out at his console with the hatch unlocked. I found a cutting tool in the maintenance locker. And I used it.
INTERNAL: He was eleven. A child with a blade and a sleeping man. That's not strength. That's what desperation looks like.
[Khalid] The others just watched. Twelve children and I was the only one who moved. The autopilot brought us to a relay station. Imperial military outpost. They took us in. Fed us. Gave us bunks.
NARRATION: Khalid's voice is flat. Like he's reading a report.
[Khalid] And then they gave us a choice. Enlist or go to the labour camps. Eleven years old. Some choice.
[Zephyr] You enlisted.
[Khalid] I enlisted. Youngest recruit in the Eighth Battalion. Worked my way up through every rank they had. Killed for them. Bled for them. And when they offered me more — when they said they could make me stronger, faster, better — I took it. You take everything when you've had nothing.
NARRATION: He goes quiet. The warp field fills the silence. His hand is still on Zephyr's forearm. Warm.
[Khalid] Now you understand the difference between you and me. Some of us submit. And some of us would rather die.
NARRATION: Zephyr says nothing. He comes around from behind the chair. Kneels in front of Khalid. Reaches for his boots. Pulls the first one off, then the second. Peels the socks away. The musk rises — familiar, heavy.
INTERNAL: It's quieter tonight. The pull. I can think. I can see him clearly — the scars, the flat voice, the boy who killed a man at eleven. And I'm still here. On my knees. Because I want to be.
[Zephyr] May I worship you, Sir?
[Khalid] Of course, boy.
NARRATION: Zephyr presses his face into Khalid's bare feet. Inhales. The smell fills him — salt, skin, something deep underneath. His tongue finds the arch. He tries to resist. His body won't let him. He licks slowly along the sole, between the toes. His eyes close. His hands grip the ankle like it's the only solid thing in the room.
[Khalid] (quiet) See. We all know our place.
NARRATION: The warp field streams past. Neither of them moves for a long time.

--- TRANSITION: slow fade on the viewport light washing over them both ---
