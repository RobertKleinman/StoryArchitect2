He walks to Khalid's chambers because his body is screaming for the musk and he can't resist anymore. That's the line.

  - Exit: Zephyr leaves with some intel but deeply shaken. His behaviour is becoming erratic. Sets up Cassius noticing in scene 7.

  Scene 2: Second exposure (suite — fought it, was denied)

  Scene 6: THIS SCENE — seeks Khalid out. No justification holds. The line between strategy and addiction is gone.

  Khalid's jealousy thread:

  - How does Khalid show tenderness? Physical (stroking hair, feeding him)? Verbal ("my pretty fighter")? Or something quieter — letting Zephyr sleep on the floor by his bed instead of sending him back to the barracks?
