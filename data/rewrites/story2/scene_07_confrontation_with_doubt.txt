SCENE 7: "Confrontation with Doubt"
Setting: Zephyr's quarters in Khalid's estate — small, spartan. A bed, a shelf, a strip light. The room is bugged — cameras, audio. Cassius knows this.
Characters: Zephyr, Cassius
POV: Zephyr

CHARACTER REFERENCE:
  Zephyr: Defensive. The pull is quieter since Erik's injection but hasn't stopped. Erik told him his blood was clear — no pheromones. So Zephyr believes what he's feeling isn't chemical. He doesn't know the injection was a counteragent. He thinks this is just him.
  Cassius: Placed himself in Khalid's household through the underground. Dressed as an attendant. Has engineered a power cut to bypass the room's surveillance. He's here as a friend, not a handler — he wants to extract Zephyr. He does NOT know Erik is underground or that Zephyr received a counteragent.

NOTE: Cassius is caught before the extraction can happen (scene 8). This is the last real conversation they have.

---

NARRATION: Zephyr's quarters. A narrow room — bed, shelf, strip light. Nothing personal. The light flickers once. Twice. Then dies. Backup lighting kicks in — dim red, emergency power. The door lock clicks open.
NARRATION: Cassius steps in. Attendant's uniform, facility ID clipped to his chest. He closes the door behind him. The red light catches the lines on his face.
[Zephyr] (surprised) Cassius?
[Cassius] We don't have long. Power will be back any minute. I cut the junction on sublevel two — bought us a window.
[Zephyr] How did you—
[Cassius] It doesn't matter. I'm resourceful. Remember?
NARRATION: Zephyr stands. Pulls him into a hug. Cassius holds on for a moment, then pushes him back. Hands on his shoulders. Looking at him.
[Cassius] We don't have time. This room is bugged. They see everything — when the power's on. I'm worried about you.
NARRATION: Zephyr sits on the edge of his bed. Something in his posture closes off.
[Zephyr] You need to leave.
[Cassius] Excuse me?
[Zephyr] I don't have anything. Nothing. He's too careful.
[Cassius] I didn't come for the intel. I came because we have to get you out of here.
[Zephyr] What are you talking about? There's no way out. You know that.
[Cassius] You don't understand. Zephyr — you're breaking. I'm not going to let that happen.
[Zephyr] I'm fine. Everything is fine. I'm in control. I'm doing exactly what I said I would.
[Cassius] No. You're not. It's what I told you before. The pheromones — they're doing something to you.
[Zephyr] There are no pheromones.
NARRATION: Cassius stops.
[Cassius] What?
[Zephyr] The medical overseer checked my blood. It's clear. There are no pheromones.
[Cassius] That's impossible. That's what they did to Hector. That's—
[Zephyr] I'm telling you what the results said. My blood is clean. Whatever this is — it's not chemicals.
NARRATION: Silence. The backup light hums. Cassius stares at him.
[Cassius] Then what the hell is wrong with you?
NARRATION: Zephyr doesn't answer. The question hangs in the red light between them.
[Cassius] (quieter) You promised me. Back in the holding cell. You promised you'd tell me before it got like Hector.
[Zephyr] It's not like Hector.
[Cassius] You're going to him willingly. Willingly, Zephyr. No one's dragging you. No one's ordering you. You just go.
[Zephyr] I'm trying to gain his trust. I can't stand it — is that what you want to hear? It's disgusting. But I do it because I believe in the cause. Just give me a chance.
[Cassius] You're lying. I can see it. The Zephyr I knew would have some dignity.
[Zephyr] (standing) Don't talk to me about dignity. You have no idea what I do in that room. What it costs me.
[Cassius] This is crazy. I'm trying to save you and you can't see it.
[Zephyr] Get out.
NARRATION: Cassius doesn't move. His jaw works. The red light flickers — the main power is coming back.
[Cassius] Three days from now. Be ready.
[Zephyr] I'm staying.
[Cassius] Three days. Just hang in there. We'll get you out. We'll get you treated.
[Zephyr] Treated for what? My blood is clear. You heard me. There's nothing to treat.
NARRATION: Cassius looks at him for a long moment. The strip light buzzes back on. Full power. The cameras are live again. Cassius straightens his attendant's uniform. Picks up a cleaning cloth from the shelf. Holds it like a prop.
[Cassius] (loud, for the room) All done in here, sir. Apologies for the delay.
NARRATION: He opens the door. Doesn't look back. The door closes. Zephyr sits on the edge of his bed. The strip light is bright and steady. The room feels smaller than before.
INTERNAL: Treated for what. If it's not chemicals, then it's me. And if it's me — what did Hector have that I don't?
