SCENE 2: "First Taste"
Setting: Khalid's suite at the arena complex — dim lighting, zero-grav loungers, panoramic window overlooking the empty pit below. Not his estate — this is the victory suite reserved for champions on fight days.
Characters: Zephyr, Khalid
POV: Zephyr

CHARACTER REFERENCE:
  Zephyr: (protagonist) Zephyr Axion, Fifth in Line, House Artemis. Hiding his identity. Just lost in the pit, now Khalid's property by arena law.
  Khalid: (antagonist) Doesn't know Zephyr is royalty yet. About to find out.

NOTE: After this scene, Zephyr is sent to the holding facility for "processing" before transfer to Khalid's estate. That's where he finds Cassius in scene 3.

---

NARRATION: Khalid reclines on a zero-grav lounger. Fresh clothes, boots laced. The suite door opens. An attendant leads Zephyr in on a leash — gold collar tight around his throat, matching cuffs on his wrists and ankles. The attendant hands Khalid the leash. Khalid stands. The attendant leaves without a word.
[Khalid] (quiet, to himself) Beautiful.
NARRATION: Zephyr says nothing. Khalid walks a slow circle around him. Touches his shoulder. Runs a hand down his arm. Inspects him like property.
NARRATION: Zephyr spits in Khalid's face.
[Zephyr] Imperial pig.
NARRATION: Khalid doesn't react. He wipes the spit off with the back of his hand. Steps behind Zephyr. Leans in close to his ear.
[Khalid] (low) Do you understand now boy? I own you. You know what that means? You're mine. All mine. And if I want, I can do anything to you.
[Zephyr] I am Zephyr Axion of House Artemis. Fifth in Line to the Ionion Kingdom. I am Zephyr Axion...
NARRATION: Khalid pauses. Then sits back down. He's amused.
[Khalid] A prince. From the rim. And here you are on a leash.
[Zephyr] I am Zephyr...
[Khalid] ENOUGH!
NARRATION: Khalid yanks the leash. Zephyr crashes to the floor.
[Khalid] Prince, peasant. Doesn't matter. You're mine now. And you're going to learn what that means.
NARRATION: Khalid pulls off his boots, peels off his socks. A vinegary musk cuts through the suite's clean air. Zephyr breathes in deeply before he can stop himself. His shoulders drop. His body starts to relax.
INTERNAL: No. Not again. Not like the pit.
NARRATION: Khalid leans forward. Puts his hand under Zephyr's chin. Tilts his face up.
[Khalid] From now on, you serve your master. You understand?
NARRATION: Khalid pulls Zephyr's face into his pit. Zephyr inhales. The musk floods in — thick, animal, the same chemical heat from the arena. His arms go limp. His eyes half-close.
INTERNAL: Stop breathing. Stop. Why does it feel like this. Why do I want more.
NARRATION: Khalid holds him there for a long moment. Then pushes him back down to the floor. Zephyr stays where he lands.
[Khalid] Now. Show me how a good boy behaves.
NARRATION: Khalid places his bare foot in front of Zephyr's face. Zephyr tries to turn away but his body won't let him. Something pulls him toward it — the smell, the warmth rising off the skin.
[Khalid] Ask permission first.
[Zephyr] (barely audible) May I?
[Khalid] May you what?
[Zephyr] May I smell your feet. Sir.
NARRATION: Khalid pulls his foot away. Stands up.
[Khalid] Good boy. See. You're learning. But not now.
[Zephyr] Please...
[Khalid] Don't worry. You'll get to know these feet better than you've known anything in your life. But not tonight.
NARRATION: Zephyr stays on the floor. His hand reaches toward where the foot was. He catches himself. Pulls it back.
NARRATION: Khalid claps twice. The door opens. An attendant steps in.
[Khalid] (to the attendant) Processing. Our prince needs to understand his place.

--- TRANSITION: cut ---
