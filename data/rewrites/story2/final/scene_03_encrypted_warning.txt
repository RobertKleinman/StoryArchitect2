SCENE 3: "Warning"
Setting: Imperial Holding Facility — processing centre in the capital. Concrete cells, strip lighting, administrative bustle beyond the walls. Zephyr is awaiting transfer to Khalid's estate.
Characters: Zephyr, Cassius
POV: Zephyr

CHARACTER REFERENCE:
  Zephyr: (protagonist) Fifth in Line, House Artemis, Ionion Kingdom. Conscripted under false identity after the raid on his planet. Now owned by Khalid.
  Cassius: (supporting) From the Ionion court, Zephyr's friend/advisor. Captured separately, now a labour slave working the holding facility. Part of the underground resistance.

WORLD CONTEXT:
  The Ionion Kingdom was raided after its ruler refused an imperial slave demand. The planet's nobility was captured or killed. Zephyr and Cassius were both taken but separated. Hector Axion (Third in Line, Zephyr's third cousin) was captured earlier and publicly broken via pheromone gene-editing — broadcast on galactic TV as a warning to other rim kingdoms. Drake and Orion were members of the underground resistance from the Ionion court — both killed in the raid or the purge that followed. The underground is a coalition of rim nobility and imperial dissidents working to overthrow the 50-year military regime.

---

NARRATION: A holding cell. Bare concrete, strip light, a metal bench bolted to the wall. Zephyr sits with his back against it, gold collar still around his neck. His wrists are raw from the cuffs. Footsteps outside. A hatch in the door slides open. A face.
[Zephyr] (surprised) Cassius?
NARRATION: The door opens. Cassius steps in — labour slave overalls, facility ID clipped to his chest. He checks the corridor behind him, then pulls the door shut.
[Cassius] Zephyr!
[Zephyr] I thought you were dead.
NARRATION: Cassius pulls him into a hug. Both of them hold on too long. When they pull apart, neither looks dry-eyed.
[Cassius] No one gets to kill the mighty Cassius.
[Zephyr] (half-smile) What happened to you? Are the others alive? Drake? Orion?
[Cassius] Dead. Both of them. The purge.
NARRATION: Zephyr closes his eyes. A beat. The strip light buzzes.
[Zephyr] (quiet) How many got out?
[Cassius] Handful. Scattered. But we're here.
[Zephyr] Yeah. We are.
[Cassius] And that means something. Listen — I don't have long. Shift change is twenty minutes.
[Zephyr] It means nothing. I belong to Khalid now. And when I'm around him I can't...
[Cassius] (sharp) Snap out of it.
[Zephyr] You don't understand.
[Cassius] No. You don't. You're closest to the second most powerful general in the empire. You know what that is?
[Zephyr] I'm his property.
[Cassius] You have access. Fucking access to elites, intel, everything we were dying for. Everything Drake and Orion died for.
[Zephyr] The revolution is dead.
NARRATION: Cassius slaps him. Hard. The sound bounces off concrete.
[Cassius] Snap out of it. What's happened to you?
INTERNAL: Tell him. Tell him about the smell, the way your body just stops listening. Tell him you wanted to stay on that floor.
[Zephyr] I don't know. It's something with my mind. I can't think straight when I'm near him. I don't know.
[Cassius] Well, snap out of it. There's people in this facility. In the barracks, in the kitchens. People who are part of the cause.
[Zephyr] You're lying.
[Cassius] You know me. I don't lie. The underground isn't dead. Not here.
[Zephyr] Tell me.
[Cassius] Not now. Just play along. Be Khalid's good boy. Get him to trust you.
NARRATION: Cassius glances at the collar around Zephyr's neck. His jaw tightens.
[Cassius] (quieter) Nice jewellery.
[Zephyr] He's a monster. And there's something else.
[Cassius] What do you mean?
[Zephyr] I don't know. It sounds crazy. Like. His smell. It does something to me.
[Cassius] Fucking freak.
[Zephyr] What?
[Cassius] I heard rumours but couldn't believe it was true. No. I believe it. Fucking disgusting.
[Zephyr] What is it? Tell me.
[Cassius] Pheromone editing. He's gene'd himself. His sweat, his musk — it rewires you. Makes you crave him.
NARRATION: Cassius's voice drops. He's not looking at Zephyr anymore.
[Cassius] Same thing they did to Hector.
INTERNAL: Hector. Third cousin. First in Line. The broadcast — him on his knees in front of General Torun, begging. Begging to be let back. Every rim planet watched. That's what they're doing to me.
[Zephyr] Trust me. I'm trying to resist. It's...
[Cassius] (cutting him off) Hey. I don't want to hear that. You can't let him win. You've got to fight it. We'll find something. There has to be something.
[Zephyr] And then we'll bring them all down.
[Cassius] That's what I like to hear.
NARRATION: Cassius grips Zephyr's shoulders. Looks him in the eye. But there's worry behind it — the kind he's trying not to show. He's seen what pheromone editing does. He watched the broadcasts like everyone else.
[Cassius] We'll bring them all down. But Zephyr — if you start slipping, you tell me. Before it gets like Hector. Promise me.
[Zephyr] I promise.
NARRATION: A buzzer sounds in the corridor. Shift change warning. Cassius lets go. Steps back.
[Cassius] I'll find you again. Stay alive. Stay sharp.
NARRATION: Cassius slips out. The door clicks shut. Zephyr sits back down. His hand goes to the collar. The gold is still warm.
INTERNAL: I promised. But in the pit, when his foot was on my face — I didn't want him to stop.

--- TRANSITION: cut ---
