SCENE 1: "The Challenge and Defeat"
Setting: The Pit
Characters: Zephyr, Khalid, Announcer
POV: Zephyr

CHARACTER REFERENCE:
  Zephyr: (protagonist) conscripted colonist, secretly royalty — hiding his identity as Fifth in Line to the Ionion Kingdom
  Khalid: (antagonist) reigning champion, massive, gene-edited

---

NARRATION: The pit roars. Thousands packed into tiered stands, stamping, chanting. The arena floor is stained dark from the last bout. A gate opens at the far end. Zephyr is shoved through it.
[Announcer] Next bout! From the conscription rolls of Outer Rim Colony Veska-3 — Zephyr! Facing our reigning champion — Lord of Touron Six and Geminon Eight, High Senator and Commander of the Eighth Battalion — Khalid Gessir the Tenth!
NARRATION: Khalid is already in the pit. Rolling his shoulders. He looks at Zephyr the way you look at something small.
[Khalid] (amused) This is what they send me? A twink from the rim?
[Zephyr] Didn't ask to be here big man.
[Khalid] Don't worry. I'll go easy on you. Wouldn't want to mess up your pretty face.
[Zephyr] (angry) Shut up.
NARRATION: Zephyr swings. Khalid catches his fist without moving his feet. Squeezes. Zephyr drops to one knee.
[Khalid] What are you? Some noble family runt? Come on. Hit me properly.
INTERNAL: If he knew. If any of them knew.
NARRATION: Khalid lets go. Zephyr scrambles up, throws a kick at Khalid's ribs. Khalid takes it. Doesn't move. Grabs Zephyr's ankle and shoves him backwards. Zephyr hits the ground.
[Zephyr] (winded) Shit.
[Khalid] That all you got?
NARRATION: Zephyr gets up. Charges. Khalid sidesteps, hooks an arm around Zephyr's waist and slams him face-first into the pit floor. Mounts him before he can roll. The crowd cheers.
[Zephyr] Get off me.
[Khalid] (whispered) Make me.
NARRATION: Zephyr shoves with everything he has. Nothing. Khalid locks in a choke hold, forearm jammed under Zephyr's nose. The smell hits — hours of sweat baked into Khalid's skin. Something chemical underneath it. Zephyr's vision blurs.
INTERNAL: What is... why can't I think.
[Zephyr] What the...
[Khalid] Shh. Don't fight it.
NARRATION: Zephyr tries to struggle. His body won't respond. His muscles have gone soft.
[Zephyr] What are you...
NARRATION: Khalid drags his tongue across Zephyr's cheek. Zephyr flinches but can't pull away.
[Khalid] (whispered) Gene-edited boy. My sweat does things to people. You're feeling it now aren't you.
[Zephyr] I...
[Khalid] Good boy. You going to yield?
[Zephyr] I...
NARRATION: Zephyr's arm goes slack. The pit falls quiet.
[Announcer] And that's a match! The winner — Khalid Gessir the Tenth!
NARRATION: Khalid stands. Flexes for the crowd. Then hauls Zephyr up by his collar and shoves him to his knees.
[Khalid] (addressing the Emperor's box) As is my right, I ask of His Greatness, my Lord and Emperor — custody of the defeated.
[Zephyr] That's not— you can't—
NARRATION: Khalid kicks Zephyr flat and plants his foot on the back of his head.
[Khalid] Show some respect.
NARRATION: Khalid lifts his foot. Zephyr grabs onto it, rolls over writhing. The Emperor rises from his box. The crowd starts chanting. He raises a finger.
NARRATION: Khalid pulls his boot off and sets his bare foot beside Zephyr's face. The smell hits again — stronger without the boot. Deep, heavy, sweet underneath the sweat. Zephyr's head swims.
[Zephyr] Please.
[Khalid] Kiss it.
[Zephyr] Please.
[Khalid] That wasn't a question.
NARRATION: Khalid grabs the back of Zephyr's head. Pushes his face down to his bare foot. Zephyr's lips touch skin — salt, heat, grit from the pit floor. The crowd erupts. Zephyr's mouth stays longer than it needs to.
[Khalid] (to the crowd) Mine now.
NARRATION: Khalid lets go. Zephyr stays on his knees. Not moving. Not sure he wants to.
[Khalid] (muttered) Gonna have a lot of fun with you boy.

--- TRANSITION: cut ---
