SCENE 9: "Loyalty's Proof"
Setting: Khalid's private chambers, then the shower. Evening after scene 8.
Characters: Zephyr, Khalid
POV: Zephyr

---

NARRATION: Khalid's chambers. A room that reminds Zephyr of the dinner on the ship — the same kind of chair, a viewport, a table. But the lights are low. No food. No warmth. Khalid sits in his chair, boots off, bare feet on the floor. He's staring at the viewport. He doesn't turn when Zephyr enters.
NARRATION: Zephyr stands by the door. His shirt is still damp from the detention cell. His hair is still wet. He smells like piss and metal.
[Khalid] (without turning) Come here.
NARRATION: Zephyr walks over. Stands beside him. Khalid reaches up and takes his hand. Pulls him down gently — not to his knees. Into the chair beside him. Zephyr sits. Khalid doesn't let go of his hand.
[Khalid] You stink.
[Zephyr] I'm sorry, Sir.
[Khalid] Come.
NARRATION: Khalid stands. Leads him by the hand through a door at the back of the chambers. A bathroom — large, tiled, steam-capable. A walk-in shower with multiple heads. Khalid turns the water on. Hot. Steam starts to fill the room.
[Khalid] Get in.
NARRATION: Zephyr strips and steps under the water. It hits his skin and the tension in his shoulders cracks. The piss, the sweat, the detention cell — it runs off him in grey rivulets.
NARRATION: Khalid pulls off his shirt. Steps in behind him. Zephyr reaches for the sponge on the shelf — instinct, service. Khalid takes it out of his hand.
INTERNAL: He's not letting me serve him. He's — what is this?
NARRATION: Khalid starts to wash Zephyr. Shoulders first. Across his back. Slow, careful strokes. The sponge is rough but his hands are steady.
[Khalid] I was angry.
[Zephyr] It's okay, Sir.
[Khalid] You're precious to me.
NARRATION: Khalid's lips press against the back of Zephyr's neck. A kiss. Not commanding. Just a mouth on skin. Zephyr closes his eyes. The steam wraps around them both.
[Zephyr] I should have told you. About the attendant.
[Khalid] (stern) Yes. You will never let this happen again.
[Zephyr] Yes, Sir. It's just...
[Khalid] Tell me.
[Zephyr] It's nothing.
[Khalid] Tell me.
[Zephyr] Do you believe me?
NARRATION: Khalid doesn't answer. He keeps washing him. The sponge moves down Zephyr's arms, across his chest. Water runs between them. The silence is its own answer — or the absence of one.
[Khalid] Who is he?
[Zephyr] Nobody, Sir.
[Khalid] Don't worry. Tell me.
[Zephyr] Nobody.
NARRATION: Khalid pauses. The sponge stops moving. He's standing close behind Zephyr — close enough that the heat between their bodies is indistinguishable from the steam.
[Khalid] I once had someone. A commandant. I fought under him in the Agentic uprising.
NARRATION: The water runs. Khalid's hands slow on the sponge.
[Khalid] You remind me of him.
[Zephyr] Why?
[Khalid] You were both weak.
[Zephyr] Sorry, Sir.
[Khalid] No.
NARRATION: Khalid hands Zephyr the sponge. Their fingers touch on the transfer. Zephyr takes it and drops to his knees on the wet tile. The water pools warm around his shins.
NARRATION: He starts at Khalid's feet. Working the sponge across the sole — slow, thorough. Between each toe. Around the ankle bone. The soap cuts the musk but it's there underneath, rising through the steam. He finishes washing. Sets the sponge on the tile. And stays where he is.
NARRATION: His lips find the arch. He holds them there. The water beats down on both of them — hot, steady, drumming on Khalid's shoulders and running down his legs to where Zephyr kneels. His tongue traces the sole. His mouth closes around the big toe, then the next. He takes his time. The tile is hard under his knees and he doesn't care.
NARRATION: When he's done, he picks up the sponge and works his way upward — calves, the backs of the knees, thighs. His hands moving over Khalid's body like he's learning it. The steam is thick enough that the walls have disappeared.
[Zephyr] I've never had anyone, Sir.
[Khalid] You have me.
[Zephyr] Yes, Sir. And I'm grateful.
[Khalid] Are you?
[Zephyr] Yes, Sir. Very much so.
NARRATION: Khalid puts his hand under Zephyr's chin. Tilts his face up. Water streams between them.
[Khalid] There are things happening. Things I can't control. Things that put everyone in this household in danger.
[Zephyr] I'll be careful, Sir.
[Khalid] No — it's for me to protect you. You and everyone here. That is my responsibility. I just need you...
[Zephyr] To what, Sir?
[Khalid] Obey.
NARRATION: The word hits Zephyr like a physical thing. Something in his chest breaks open. He drops the sponge. His hands go to Khalid's sides. He buries his face in Khalid's armpit — pressing in, inhaling, his mouth on the skin. The musk is concentrated here — the water hasn't reached it, trapped in the hollow. Salt, heat, the deep animal scent underneath. His tongue drags across the hair and skin. His body shudders.
INTERNAL: The pull is quiet. It's been quiet for days. Erik's injection — whatever it was — something changed. So what is this? What is this thing that hears "obey" and stops thinking? My blood is clear. Whatever Erik gave me is working. And I'm on my knees in a shower licking his armpit because he said one word. One word.
[Zephyr] (muffled against skin) Whatever you want, Sir.
NARRATION: Khalid's hand comes up and cradles the back of Zephyr's head. Holding him there. The water runs over them both. Neither speaks.
NARRATION: Somewhere below them, the squalid pulses around Cassius's head. Pulling memories. Giving bliss. And Zephyr is here, in the steam, doing the same thing to himself.
