NARRATION: Holo-screens cast jagged light on the alcove walls. Zephyr leans against a damp terminal console, sleeve still carrying arena sand. {Screens flicker with senate vote tallies.}
[RAFAEL ESPINOZA] (motivated) Cassius, this slip in the pit—crowd ate it up, but Khalid's not blind. We turn that heat into our blade, yeah? Who's the new face? {Gestures at Tomas Ruiz, who stands rigid by the door panel.}
[YOON DOWAN] (measured) (gravelly, archaic phrasing) Tomas Ruiz. Fought under my banner in the Epsilon uprising. Lost his squad to senate asset grabs. Now he tallies the ledgers they don't want seen. {Nods to Tomas, who pulls a slim data chit from his vest pocket.}
[TOMAS RUIZ] (tense) Records show champions' vetoes routed through shell corps. Khalid's next vote—it's primed for a freeze if we tag the right relay. {Slides the chit across the console; it skids into a puddle of condensation.}
INTERNAL (distracted): Chit gleams wet. Like the pit floor under his boot. Focus, Raf—relay codes, not the taste.
[RAFAEL ESPINOZA] (hyper_focused) Relay? Which one—the prime hub or those fringe backups? We hit wrong, it's exile tags for all— {Fingers drum the console edge, speeding up.}
[YOON DOWAN] (pessimistic) (fragmenting) Patience carves the stone. Prime hub's watched. Fringes... {Trails off as the alcove door hisses open.}
NARRATION: Erik Lindqvist steps in, overseer badge clipped loose on his collar. He doesn't sit. A loose wire dangles from his gauntlet. {Door seals with a dull thud.}
[ERIK LINDQUIST] (clipped) (official, with reluctant edge) Protocol 47-C: no unscheduled clusters in shadow zones. But your relay query—fringes link to my tower logs. Encrypted slice, one-time. {Tosses a micro-drive; it bounces once before Zephyr snags it.}
[RAFAEL ESPINOZA] (sarcastic) (quick-witted lilt) Tower logs? Erik, you're handing us Khalid's veto printout? That's—wait, one-time? What if the encryption pings back? {Pockets the drive, but eyes flick to Tomas's clenched fist on the chit.}
[TOMAS RUIZ] (angry) It'll ping. Always does. My squad got fragged on a 'one-time' tip. You gladiators play submissive—you don't last. {Snatches the wet chit back, wiping it on his thigh.}
INTERNAL (conflicted): Tomas's thigh—sweat-dark fabric. Not now. But the drive's warm in my pocket, like skin.
[YOON DOWAN] (stressed) (muttering, trailing) Uprisings teach— {Cuts off as Erik gestures sharply.}
[ERIK LINDQUIST] (tense)  {Points to the lift panel without a word.}
NARRATION: Silence stretches. Lift doors grind open. No one moves first. {Terminals hum louder in the gap.}
[RAFAEL ESPINOZA] (resolute) (sarcastic quip cut short) Tower it is, then. Show us the logs, Overseer. But if this is a rule bend that snaps— {Steps into the lift; others follow unevenly.}
NARRATION: Lift ascends with a jolt. Command Tower viewscreen shows the Colosseum Pit below, sand still churned from the match. {Erik keys a side panel; arena records scroll—Khalid's veto tallies highlighted.}
[TOMAS RUIZ] (insistent) See? Shell corp 9-Alpha. Pull that, his next vote dies. But exposure risk—your champion's aides get scanned post-match. {Jabs the screen; highlight flickers.}
[ERIK LINDQUIST] (bureaucratic) Scan protocols optional. Log it as maintenance. {Turns away, collar clip finally falling to the floor.}
[YOON DOWAN] (pessimistic) (fragmented) Optional leads to— {Lift dings; doors open to Tower corridor.}
INTERNAL (torn): Logs burn in my grip. Leverage on him. Or kneel deeper. The sand calls.
[RAFAEL ESPINOZA] (empowered) Got it. Shell 9-Alpha. We're armed. {Pockets both chit and drive, turning toward the exit ramp.}
NARRATION: Group disperses into Tower shadows. Zephyr's boot prints arena sand on the grated floor. {Ramp leads down to Barracks transit.}

--- TRANSITION: Cut to Zephyr descending alone, micro-drive heavy in his pocket. ---
