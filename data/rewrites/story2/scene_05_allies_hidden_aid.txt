SCENE 5: "Allies and Hidden Aid"
Setting: Medical bay at Khalid's estate — sterile, clinical. Examination table, cabinets, monitoring equipment. The room is monitored.
Characters: Zephyr, Erik Lindqvist
POV: Zephyr

CHARACTER REFERENCE:
  Zephyr: Installed in Khalid's household. Playing the obedient slave per Cassius's instructions. The addiction is getting worse — he's actively fighting cravings between encounters with Khalid. After scene 4's public display, he's rattled. The resistance mark from the Ionion court is hidden under his ribs.
  Erik Lindqvist: Veteran arena overseer. Early 50s. Conducts medical processing of arena fighters and household slaves — part of his official duties. Clipped, official tone. Goes silent under pressure. Part of the underground resistance — recognises the Ionion court mark.

NOTE: This room is monitored. Everything Erik says aloud is calibrated for whoever is listening. His real communication happens through actions and the code phrase.

---

NARRATION: A medical bay. White walls, strip lighting, the hum of monitoring equipment. An examination table in the centre. Erik Lindqvist stands by a cabinet, reviewing a data pad. An attendant leads Zephyr in.
[Erik] (without looking up) Standard processing. You'll need to undress.
NARRATION: Erik gestures at the attendant without raising his eyes from the pad.
[Erik] That'll be all. Full physical. I'll call when he's ready.
NARRATION: The attendant leaves. The door seals. Zephyr pulls off his shirt, then the rest. Stands in the cold light. Erik sets the pad down and begins a slow circuit around him. Clinical. Detached. Checking joints, muscle condition, skin. His fingers press along Zephyr's ribcage. He stops.
[Erik] (flat) You are... that mark.
NARRATION: Erik's fingers hover over the small raised scar below Zephyr's left arm. Deliberate pattern. Not a wound. He leans closer.
[Zephyr] It means nothing.
NARRATION: Erik says nothing for a long moment. Then straightens. His face gives nothing away.
[Erik] On the table.
NARRATION: Zephyr climbs onto the examination table. Erik continues the physical — pulse, reflexes, eyes, throat. Methodical. Then he walks to the medical cabinet and opens it. Pulls out vials. Starts mixing.
[Zephyr] Is something wrong?
[Erik] Nothing. Standard.
NARRATION: Erik draws the mixture into an injector. Taps the air out. Turns back toward Zephyr.
[Zephyr] What is that?
NARRATION: Zephyr slides off the table. Backs up a step.
[Zephyr] You're not injecting me with anything.
[Erik] Don't make this harder than it needs to be.
[Zephyr] What is it? Tell me.
[Erik] (silence)
[Zephyr] Tell me! I have a right to know. I have at least that.
NARRATION: Erik walks toward the comm panel on the wall. Zephyr moves between him and the panel.
[Zephyr] Please. You don't understand.
[Erik] I don't understand? You think I want to do this?
[Zephyr] I'm Ionion nobility. Don't do this to me.
[Erik] (quiet) I know who you are. And it doesn't change anything.
[Zephyr] Then help me. The pheromones — I know what they are. Make them stop.
[Erik] (pause) Pheromones?
[Zephyr] His feet. His sweat. They do something to me. To my brain. It has to be illegal. Even the Empire doesn't allow gene-editing on this scale.
[Erik] (sharp) Keep quiet. Do you have any idea what would happen to you if anyone else heard those accusations?
[Zephyr] It's true.
[Erik] It doesn't matter what's true. This is Khalid's house. Everyone here — everyone — is his. And there are no pheromones. Your blood is clear.
[Zephyr] What?
NARRATION: Erik holds his gaze. Steady. Giving nothing.
[Zephyr] It can't be.
NARRATION: Erik takes Zephyr's arm. Firm grip. Injects him before Zephyr can pull away. Zephyr flinches. It's done.
[Zephyr] Why?
[Erik] (silence)
[Zephyr] Why?
[Erik] (low) The blue bird will fly.
NARRATION: Zephyr goes still. The words hang in the sterile air. Erik holds his gaze for one second. Then turns, walks to the comm panel, and presses it.
[Erik] (official tone) He's done. You can come in.
NARRATION: Erik doesn't look at Zephyr again.
[Erik] Get dressed.
NARRATION: Zephyr pulls his clothes back on. His hands are shaking. Not from the injection. The attendant enters. Erik is already writing on his data pad. The door opens. Zephyr is led out.
INTERNAL: The blue bird will fly. Cassius's words. He's one of ours. And whatever he just put in my arm — it wasn't standard.
