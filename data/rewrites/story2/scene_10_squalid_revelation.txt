SCENE 10: "The Extraction"
Setting: Khalid's chambers (opening), then the detention cell. The squalid is still attached to Cassius. Erik has been running the extraction for hours.
Characters: Zephyr, Khalid, Erik Lindqvist, Cassius (unconscious/in bliss)
POV: Zephyr

---

NARRATION: Khalid's chambers. Early hours. Zephyr is on the floor beside the bed — a blanket pulled over him, his head near Khalid's hand which hangs off the edge. He's half-asleep. The comm chimes. Khalid stirs. Answers.
[Erik] (over comm, clipped) My Lord. You need to see this. Both of you. Immediately.
NARRATION: Khalid looks down at Zephyr. Pulls on his boots.
[Khalid] Get up. Come.
NARRATION: The detention cell. The squalid is wrapped around Cassius's head and torso — pulsing slowly, its translucent body flickering with faint light. Cassius hangs limp in the chains. His breathing is deep, rhythmic. The low moan from before is gone — he's past sound now. Just a body being read.
NARRATION: Erik stands at a console. A holoscreen projects a murky image — shapes, voices, fragments. A memory being reconstructed.
[Erik] The extraction hit a deep cluster. Something I thought you'd want to see. Immediately.
[Khalid] What about the rest? Twenty hours — what did we get?
[Erik] The subject's knowledge of any network is limited. Compartmentalised cells — he knew contacts, not structure. Names of a few operatives, meeting locations that are likely already burned. Nothing actionable on a larger scale.
[Khalid] Useless.
[Erik] Except for this.
[Khalid] Show me.
NARRATION: Erik taps the console. The holoscreen sharpens slightly. A room — high-ceilinged, ornate. Figures seated at a long table. Across from them: two figures in unmarked military uniforms. No insignia. No name tags. One of the figures is partially visible — tall, broad-shouldered, a face that could be Titus.
[Voice in memory] (fragmented, distorted) ...arrangement... shared... understanding... guarantee...
[Khalid] (very still) What is this?
[Erik] Buried memories. Deliberately suppressed — or possibly suppressed for him. I'm not aware of any technology that can do that, but that's what it would seem.
[Khalid] Fix it. I want to know what else is there.
[Erik] The cluster is fragmented. I can enhance the reconstruction, but the squalid is already showing signs of metabolic stress. It's been attached for over twenty hours.
[Khalid] Yes. Do it.
[Erik] (pause) There's a chance the squalid won't survive the strain. Once it dies, the extraction ends. Whatever hasn't been pulled is lost.
[Khalid] Then we try again with another one.
[Erik] It's a one-shot process. The subject's brain has already adapted to the squalid's neural pattern. A second creature won't take.
[Khalid] Is there anything else in the extraction?
[Erik] It's stuck on the cluster. We can bypass it, but the cluster will be lost for good.
[Khalid] Enhance it.
[Erik] Your Eminence. As you wish.
NARRATION: Erik turns to the console. His fingers move across the controls — frequency adjustments, bioelectric stimulation, neural gain pushed past the safety threshold. The squalid's body ripples. Its tentacles tighten around Cassius. The pulsing accelerates.
NARRATION: The holoscreen floods with images — faster now, fragmented. The room with the uniformed figures. Star charts. A face — closer now, unmistakably Titus. His mouth moving.
[Voice in memory] (clearer, then breaking apart) ...Empire... Declion Four... trust... fools... foolish...
[Khalid] Enhance it more. It's gibberish.
NARRATION: Erik adjusts the panels. The squalid's body is rippling faster now — the translucent flesh darkening at the edges. A warning tone sounds from the console. Erik silences it.
[Erik] The engrams are deteriorating. We need to stop this now.
[Khalid] No. Go on.
NARRATION: Erik pushes the gain higher. The holoscreen flickers violently — images stacking, bleeding into each other. Titus's face dissolves. The Ionion court dissolves. The fragments scatter into noise.
NARRATION: The squalid convulses. Its body goes rigid. The translucent flesh turns opaque — white, then grey. The pulsing stops. A high-pitched sound fills the cell, then cuts to silence.
NARRATION: The creature slides off Cassius and hits the floor. Dead. A puddle of slime spreads across the drain grate. The holoscreen freezes on the last reconstructed frame — a partial star chart with Titus's seal in the corner.
NARRATION: Cassius hangs in the chains. His body sags. His eyes are half-open but there's nothing behind them. His mouth moves without sound. His fingers twitch in a slow, rhythmic pattern — reaching for something that isn't there anymore.
INTERNAL: He's gone. The bliss took everything and now there's nothing left. He'll spend the rest of his life reaching for the squalid's touch. Just like Hector reaches for Torun. Just like I reach for —
[Erik] (scanning Cassius) Subject's higher functions are severely compromised. Motor response minimal. Cognitive assessment will take days, but the prognosis is consistent with standard squalid separation outcomes.
[Khalid] Forget the slave. What did we get on Titus?
[Erik] Partial intelligence. The enhancement pulled the core cluster before the squalid failed. Titus was recruiting rim kingdoms for a coordinated action against the Emperor. At least three years of groundwork. Names, locations, a logistics chain. Fragmentary but significant.
[Khalid] (quiet, dangerous) Three years. That bastard's been building this for three years. And nobody knew.
NARRATION: Khalid stares at the frozen star chart. Then turns away. Walks to the door. Stops beside Zephyr.
NARRATION: His hand comes up and rests on the back of Zephyr's neck. Squeezes once. Hard. Then gentle.
[Khalid] (from the doorway) Clear this up.
NARRATION: He's gone. The door stays open. Erik stands at the console. Zephyr stands in the middle of the room. Neither speaks.
NARRATION: Zephyr looks at Cassius. The empty eyes. The twitching fingers. The mouth moving without sound. He steps closer. Cassius doesn't react. Doesn't recognise him. The man who hugged him in the holding cell, who slapped him awake, who said "we'll bring them all down" — gone.
[Zephyr] (whispered) I'm sorry.
NARRATION: Cassius's fingers keep reaching.
NARRATION: Erik turns off the holoscreen. The star chart disappears. The cell goes dim.
[Erik] (without turning around, barely audible) The blue bird will fly.
NARRATION: Zephyr looks at Erik's back. Erik doesn't turn. The moment passes.
[Zephyr] (whispered) It will.
NARRATION: Zephyr walks out. The corridor is empty. Behind him, the door seals.
